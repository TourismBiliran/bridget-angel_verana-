About Tomalistis Falls

Tomalistis Falls is a hidden gem located in Brgy. Tomalistis, Caibiran, Biliran. Tucked within lush greenery and a serene forest setting, the falls cascade gracefully down natural limestone rocks into a clear, cool basin — a refreshing sight and experience for nature lovers and adventurers alike. What makes Tomalistis Falls truly unique is its reputation for having one of the cleanest natural spring waters in the Philippines. In fact, the Department of Environment and Natural Resources (DENR) once recognized its water as among the cleanest inland bodies of water in the country.

Reserve a place

The once untouched natural beauty has evolved to welcome more visitors, with charming cottages now perched on the rocks and a boardwalk built for easier access to the waterfalls. For those seeking peace and privacy, floating cottages are available to rent—perfect for quietly soaking in the serene views away from the crowd.

You can also rent a kayak for just P100 per hour and enjoy a leisurely paddle around the area. Don’t miss the chance to snorkel and discover the stunning corals and colorful fish beneath the crystal-clear brackish water. But be cautious—sea urchins are common here.

HOW TO GET THERE: An hour land trip to Brgy. Asug from Naval. Then 5-minutes walk from the entrance going to the waterfalls. Rates

ENTRANCE FEE: Php 20
COTTAGE FEE: depends on the size, Php 500-800. Floating cottage price ranges from Php 800 – Php 1,200.

What to Bring: Food
